Essential Equipment

At Crossfit Tweedbank, we use a variety of equipment to perform functional movements and high-intensity workouts. Some essential equipment includes:

  • Barbells and weights
  • Kettlebells
  • Medicine balls
  • Jump ropes
  • Plyometric boxes

Additional Equipment

In addition to the essential equipment, we also utilize various accessories and gear to enhance our workouts, such as:

  • Resistance bands
  • Rowing machines
  • Assault bikes
  • Pull-up bars
  • Box jumps
