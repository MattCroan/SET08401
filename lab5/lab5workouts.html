Types of Workouts

At Crossfit Tweedbank, we participate in a variety of workouts designed to improve strength, endurance, and overall fitness. Some common types of workouts include:

  • High-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Strength training
  • Cardiovascular conditioning
  • Functional movements

Sample Workouts

Here are a few examples of typical Crossfit workouts:

  • AMRAP (As Many Rounds As Possible)
  • EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute)
  • Chipper
